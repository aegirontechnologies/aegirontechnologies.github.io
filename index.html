What is VIREON?

VIREON is an open modular CPU platform, featuring socketed ARMv9 and RISC-V processors designed for scalable, secure, and ethical computing across consumer, enterprise, and defense sectors.

Introducing Emotion²

Inspired by the PS3's Cell processor, Emotion² is a modern RISC-V architecture combining a scalar control core with modular AI/vector tiles. Ideal for AI, simulation, gaming, and secure edge compute.

Socket Standard: LGA-Z1

The LGA-Z1 socket supports up to 1056 pins and enables upgradable CPU modules from 4 to 32 cores (consumer) and up to 196 cores (server). It empowers motherboard manufacturers and developers to build custom open hardware platforms.

Behavioral Integrity: BIEM

BIEM (Behavioral Integrity Enhancement Module) is a dedicated PCIe module designed to enforce ethical and logical safeguards at the hardware level. It ensures AI systems and critical infrastructure operate within defined behavioral boundaries—supporting defense, industrial, and community deployments.

AI Acceleration: VIREON Nova TPU

The VIREON Nova TPU is a scalable AI accelerator designed to enhance FP16/INT8 inference workloads. Available in modular form factors, it supports AI processing at the edge and in data centers, complementing Emotion² compute nodes and BIEM governance layers.
